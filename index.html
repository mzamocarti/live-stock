How it works

Crowdfarming is a simple way to help anyone buy & own a real profit-earning assets, helping farmers grow and maintain assets until ready for the market

  1. You buy the young asset(livestock or plant-based) for the farmer, through LivestockWealth
  2. The asset grows in the farmers care
  3. The farmer buys back the asset once it has fully grown at harvest. You, the investor, make a profit from thw sale
